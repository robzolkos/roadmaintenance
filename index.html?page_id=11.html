Products

Road Maintenance Pty Ltd products available throughout Australia:

  • Maxi-Seal (Class 320 Bitumen) Polymer Rubberised Bitumen for Crack Sealing
  • Crumb Rubberised Bitumen for spray sealing, all Grades –  S45R,  20 Parts, 18 Parts, 15 Parts, 10 Parts and 5 Parts.

Maxi-Seal is a polymer based joint and crack sealing material offering excellent adhesion and performance properties for use on asphalt and concrete pavements.   Road Maintenance Pty Ltd manufactures Maxi-Seal using Class 320 bitumenwhich achieves better coverage, longer life and is also tested in our NATA Accredited Laboratory. Maxi-Seal is designed to withstand both high and low levels of the weather spectrum. This product will not bleed through after re-surfacing.
